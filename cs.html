How scoring works (12 questions, 100 points)

Each closed ticket is scored against a 12-question rubric covering four sections.

  • A. Technical Accuracy & Retention (35 pts): Solution accuracy, retention SOP adherence, tool usage.
  • B. Documentation & Tagging (30 pts): Inquiry tag, resolution tag, internal notes, macro customization.
  • C. Communication & Voice (25 pts): Tone/empathy, clarity, opening/closing.
  • D. Efficiency (10 pts): First contact resolution, conciseness.

Scoring is pattern-matching on ticket text and tags, so results are best used to spot trends across tickets rather than judge any single reply. Reliable categories: tags, notes, FCR, conciseness. Proxy categories: solution accuracy, tone, retention tier.

Retention Attempts KPI — ratio of cancel/return tickets where the agent actually tried to save the customer. A ticket is counted as a retention ticket if it carries one of these Gorgias tags: cancellation request, return request, or subs_cancel. It counts as attempted when the agent's reply contains at least one retention keyword from this list: discount, % off, coupon, free, offer, save, keep, extend, pause, skip, move your, next shipment, would you like, we can offer, as a valued customer, one-time, credit, partial refund. The card shows attempted / retention tickets.
