Source & methodology
Reiner's CB sheet
Case volume: which CBs happened, when. Filtered to fightable platforms (Paypal / Stripe Dispute / Payarc / Aurora / EMS). Brand from RMD LC or NR column.
CheckoutChamp (CC)
Field orderType — tells us if the disputed charge was a subscription rebill or a fresh sale.
orderType = RECURRING → rebill. Any other value → new-sale.
Why we need CC: the sheet doesn't carry a rebill/new-sale flag. CC is the authoritative source for order type.
Join key
Email match: Sheet.Email ↔ CC.emailAddress + amount + date proximity. Match rate ~63% of fightable CBs; unmatched cases default to new-sale.
